Reconciling Bank Statements
You can reconcile your BeFinancial data with your bank statements to assure that you have entered all of your transactions correctly and have account for all of your payments and deposits.  This is very much similar to balancing your checkbook as one would do by hand.

To reconcile an account, select it from the list box at the bottom right, and then press the Reconcile Button.  The Reconcile View shown above will replace the Categorization Graph.  The Reconcile View allows you to enter the starting and ending balance as shown on the transaction.  It will then show you your correspondence to the statement balance as you reconcile transactions from your statement by double-clicking on the entries in the Register View.

As you double-click on items in the Register View, they will show a checkmark on the right side of the entry.  When you have completed entering the transactions shown on your balance, and the amount of deposits and payments concurs with the balances you have given, the Finish Button will be enabled.  Push the Finish Button and the entries with checkmarks will have their checkmarks change to checkmarks within boxes, symbolizing that these transactions have been reconciled to a previous statement (the one you just did).
